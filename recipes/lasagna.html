Beef lasagna recipe

This is a picture of a delicious lasagna that you can make

Follow this recipe and you can make it just like this

Ingredients

  • Beef/lentils
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Cook the lentils/beef
  2. Simmer with tomatoes and basil for 30 mins
  3. Layer with lasagna sheets
  4. Put cheese on and grill
